BoatSpotMedia v50.10 Fix Delete Incomplete Batches
Base: v50.9.
Fix:
- Adds working POST route /creator/clean-incomplete-batches.
- Also supports /creator/batches/clean-incomplete.
- Deletes/soft-deletes only incomplete, failed, uploading, cancelled, draft, or empty batches for the logged-in creator.
- Completed batches with active videos are not deleted.
- Recalculates creator storage after cleanup.
Does not touch payments, checkout, uploads, downloads, analytics, Stripe, subscriptions, My Orders, logins, or R2.

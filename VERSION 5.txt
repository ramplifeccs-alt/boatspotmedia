BoatSpotMedia v44.3 Bundle Multi-Delivery Fix
Base: v44.2.
Fix:
- Bundle/combo appears as one purchased video with two deliveries:
  * Original Clip / Instant Download
  * Edited Version / Pending Edit or Download Edited Video
- Original and edited have separate buttons and separate 72-hour timers.
- Combo keeps original downloadable immediately while edited remains pending until creator uploads it.
- Compatible with old orders where original and edited were stored as separate order items: groups them visually by video_id.
- Download route supports ?delivery=original or ?delivery=edited for bundle rows.

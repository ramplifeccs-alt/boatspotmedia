Before choosing a paid plan

Test the platform first: Your free plan includes 5GB. Upload 1 or 2 short sample videos first to confirm your video format, preview, search, pricing, checkout, and download flow work correctly before paying for more storage.

Stripe processing fees: Stripe charges payment processing fees on successful card payments. Standard U.S. online card pricing is currently 2.9% + 30¢ per successful domestic card transaction. International cards and currency conversion may cost more. These Stripe fees are separate from the BoatSpotMedia commission.

Video sales commission: BoatSpotMedia keeps the commission shown on your plan. Your payout is the remaining amount after the platform commission and Stripe/Connect processing costs.

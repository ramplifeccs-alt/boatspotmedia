BoatSpotMedia v44.5 Creator Sales Panel
Base: v44.4.
Adds:
- Read-only Sales / Orders panel in Creator Dashboard.
- Shows recent sold videos, package, price, thumbnail, order number.
- Shows summary cards:
  * Gross sales
  * Items sold
  * Pending edits
  * Discount approvals
- Adds quick actions:
  * Upload Edit for pending edited purchases
  * Approve / Reject for pending discounts
Does not touch:
- Upload batches
- Existing uploader/R2 pipeline
- Existing pricing logic

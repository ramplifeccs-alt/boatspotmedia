BoatSpotMedia v50.4K Auto Trim Free Storage
Base: v50.4J.
Adds:
- When Stripe sends customer.subscription.deleted:
  1. creator is downgraded to Free / 5GB
  2. system trims storage to the Free limit
  3. keeps newest videos first
  4. deletes oldest unsold videos first
  5. protects purchased/order-linked videos
  6. schedules R2 deletion for removed video files/thumbnails/previews
Does not touch normal uploads, batches logic, analytics backend, purchase checkout, Stripe Connect payouts, downloads, My Orders, or logins.

BoatSpotMedia v50.4I Creator Cancel/Resume Subscription
Base: v50.4H.
Adds:
- Cancel Subscription button on creator Billing.
- Resume Subscription button when cancellation is scheduled.
- Stripe Subscription.modify(cancel_at_period_end=True/False).
- Saves cancel_at_period_end/current_period_end/status in creator_subscription.
- Keeps access active until Stripe current_period_end.
Does not touch R2, uploads, batches logic, analytics backend, payments checkout flow, Stripe Connect payouts, downloads, My Orders, or logins.

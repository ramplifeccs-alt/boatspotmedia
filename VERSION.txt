BoatSpotMedia v38.8 Safe Incomplete Only Delete Fix
Base: v38.8 Floating Ghost Cleanup Button.
- The button no longer deletes the latest good batch.
- It deletes only batches that are incomplete/cancelled/failed/uploading, empty, or without valid active video keys.
- If no incomplete batch exists, it returns: No incomplete batch found. Nothing was deleted.
- Good batches with active videos are protected.
- Does not touch login, dashboard, upload logic, search, thumbnails, time/date, or logo.

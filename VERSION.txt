BoatSpotMedia v50.5AK Homepage Advertising Campaigns
Base: v50.5AJ.

Adds a complete Owner-controlled homepage advertising banner system.

New table:
- homepage_ad_campaign

Owner:
- /owner/homepage-ads
- Create campaign
- Upload banner image
- Set advertiser name/email
- Set target link
- Set price
- Set start/end dates
- Activate/deactivate
- Open in new tab
- Display order
- Preview/payment link
- Mark paid manually if needed
- Delete campaign

Public:
- /sponsored/<token>
- advertiser previews campaign
- advertiser accepts Advertising Terms & Conditions
- advertiser pays with Stripe Checkout
- after payment, Stripe webhook marks campaign paid and active
- banner appears automatically on homepage during active paid dates
- expired banners disappear automatically

Homepage:
- Shows paid/active/scheduled banners after the hero search section.
- No banner appears if no active paid campaign exists.

Does not use or modify:
- user table
- creator_profile
- creator_application
- video sales
- creator payouts
- support messages
- uploads
- pricing
- billing logic

Advertising terms are embedded in the public campaign payment page.

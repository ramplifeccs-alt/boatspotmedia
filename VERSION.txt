BoatSpotMedia v50.5Z Owner Creator Rules Panel
Base: v50.5T.

Adds:
- Owner dashboard creator rules page.
- No hardcoded creator IDs.
- No new creator tables.
- Uses creator_profile as operational creator table.
- Uses creator_subscription for internal/subscription exemption.
- Adds Rules button under Owner > Creators.

Owner can set per creator:
- No subscription required / internal creator
- Storage limit GB
- Video commission override %
- Video commission override expiration
- Product commission override %
- Product commission override expiration
- Reasons for overrides

Does not touch uploads, R2, batches, checkout, billing UI, Stripe checkout, payouts, support, Google OAuth, or SMS.

BoatSpotMedia v50.4J Webhook + Auto Downgrade
Base: v50.4I.
Adds:
- Stripe webhook endpoint:
  POST /creator/stripe/webhook
  POST /creator/billing/stripe-webhook
- Handles customer.subscription.updated:
  syncs cancel_at_period_end, current_period_end, status, customer/subscription IDs.
- Handles customer.subscription.deleted:
  auto downgrades creator to Free plan, 5GB storage, cancel_at_period_end false.
- Fixes DB update WHERE so cancel_at_period_end always saves by creator_id.
- Keeps paid plan access until Stripe sends deleted event at end of period.
Does not touch R2, uploads, batches logic, analytics backend, purchase checkout, Stripe Connect payouts, downloads, My Orders, or logins.

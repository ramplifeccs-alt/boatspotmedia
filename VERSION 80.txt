BoatSpotMedia v50.5C Internal Support Center
Base: v50.5B.

Adds internal professional support system:
- Buyer Support: buyers can request support from the creator for specific orders.
- Creator Support Center:
  - Buyer Messages
  - Platform Support
- Owner Support Inbox: owner responds to creator platform support.
- Chat-style thread history with replies.
- Threads are separated by buyer/creator/order and creator/owner.

New tables auto-created:
- support_thread
- support_message

Does not touch uploads, R2, batches, checkout, billing, Stripe, payouts, analytics, Google OAuth, or Twilio SMS.

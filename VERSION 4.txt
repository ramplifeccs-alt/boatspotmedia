BoatSpotMedia v44.2 Visible Timer + Safe Batch Delete
Base: v44.1.
Adds/Fixes:
- Buyer sees a live countdown timer for each 72-hour download window.
- Expired download buttons disable automatically in the UI.
- Adds safe batch delete endpoint:
  /creator/batches/<batch_id>/safe-delete
- Safe delete keeps purchased/active videos and deletes only safe/non-active videos.
- Creator gets a message explaining what was deleted and what was protected.
- Active means: download window still active, edit pending/active, or discount approval pending.

BoatSpotMedia v50.4O Buyer Auth Upgrade
Base: v50.4N.
Adds:
- Buyer login/register UI with tabs.
- Buyer registration stores phone_number and sms_notifications_enabled in user table.
- Forgot password flow with secure token and 1-hour expiry.
- Password reset email via SendGrid.
- SMS-ready buyer fields for edited video notifications.
- Apple login hidden/removed from buyer UI.
- Google login remains visible at /auth/google/buyer.
Does not touch R2, uploads, batches logic, analytics, billing, Stripe, checkout, downloads, My Orders, or payouts.
